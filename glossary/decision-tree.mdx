## Decision tree

A <Link href="/glossary#supervised-learning">supervised learning</Link> algorithm that constructs a tree-like structure composed of conditions at each split.
* The *root* is where the first decision is made.
* A *decision node* are decisions that split the data based on features.
* A *leaf* is an output or prediction.
